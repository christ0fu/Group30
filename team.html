Ideal Jobs



  • App developer (Chris)

  • Solution Architect (Edwin)

  • cloud engineer (Brendan)

  • front end developer (Mark, ash)

  • senior systems engineer (Kim)



Within Technodrome inc., 3 of our members would like to be in a developer type role. Developer roles are creative positions that build and create software and applications. Engineer roles where chosen by 2 members and are similar in that they are a creative position, but apply different principles to their work. An engineer will be responsible for designing, building and maintaining software and services, while a developer is working to improve and develop existing software. An architect, chosen by Edwin, Is another similar role as is also a very creative heavy role. An architect may not be responsible for the labour of coding specific applications or features like a developer may be, but will have included them on the design for a project.

A very clear management structure can be seen within Technodrome inc. starting at the top will be the project architect, Edwin who will design a project blueprint. The engineers such as Kim and Brendan will design sections of the blueprint and plans for them, and the development team, Chris, Mark and Ash will work actively on creating said plans and designs through programming.

A lot of the roles share similar duties and common skillsets, as can be seen in our section Industry Data (link).
